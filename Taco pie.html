Recipe for taco pie

  2. 1 cup salsa

  4. 1 (8.5 ounce) package corn bread mix

  6. 1 egg
  7. 1 tablespoon honey
  8. 1 tablespoon corn oil
  9. ½ cup corn (Optional)
  10. 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  11. 1 cup corn chips, partially crushed
